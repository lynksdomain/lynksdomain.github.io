Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 5, 2026

Fleck is a private micro-journal. Your entries are yours — they live on your device, and we built Fleck so we can't read them.

Your entries stay yours

Everything you write in Fleck — your mood, notes, feeling tags, and any photos — is stored on your device. If you're signed in to iCloud, Fleck syncs it privately across your devices using Apple's iCloud (CloudKit), into your own private iCloud account.

That data is stored in your iCloud space and is not accessible to us. Fleck has no account, no login, and no server that holds your journal. We never see your entries.

AI insights — only if you turn them on

AI insights are off by default and require your explicit opt-in. If you enable them, the text and mood of the relevant entries are sent over a secure connection to our AI provider (OpenAI) to generate your insight.

Your entries are never used to train AI models. The provider may retain the request briefly to guard against abuse, then deletes it. We also send a random, anonymous identifier (not tied to your name or account) purely to prevent abuse of the service.

You can turn AI off at any time in Settings. With it off, nothing from your journal ever leaves your device. Photos are never sent for AI processing. Insights are meant for reflection — they are not medical or mental-health advice.

What we don't do

  • No accounts, no email or sign-up required.
  • No advertising and no ad trackers.
  • We never sell or share your data.
  • No third-party analytics or usage tracking.

Purchases

Optional Premium subscriptions and tips are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat, which tell Fleck whether a purchase is active. We do not receive your payment details. Purchases are tied to your Apple ID, not to any Fleck account.

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled locally on your device. They never leave it, and they're entirely optional.

Deleting your data

Delete any entry inside Fleck and it's removed from your device and your iCloud. You can export all of your entries at any time from Settings. Removing the app, or turning off iCloud for Fleck, removes the synced copy per Apple's iCloud controls.

Children

Fleck is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes & contact

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Questions about privacy? Email us at lynksdomain@gmail.com.
